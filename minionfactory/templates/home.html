Get To Know Us!

With the world population on the rise every year, the amount of waste we produce annually is also growing at an exponential rate. Out of these mountains of garbage, nearly 34 billion pounds is recyclable cloth waste. Keeping in mind this treasure trove that we toss out every year, we have embarked on a mission to rejuvinate the waste cloth and shoes dumpped in our landfills every year, and give them a new life.

Here at Green Shoeniverse, we make wonders of waste by collecting nearly 13 million tonnes of waste cloth from different landfills accross the country and recycling them into stunning pair of footwear for you to stay in your comfort zone, in style!
